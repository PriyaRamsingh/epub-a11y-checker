Example 1: Fails because the background color is not specified.

Example 2: Fails because the ratio is too low (3.59:1) for even large font sizes. A ratio of 4.5:1 is required.

Example 3: Fails because the ratio is too low (6.29:1) for a normal font size. A ratio of 7:1 is required.
