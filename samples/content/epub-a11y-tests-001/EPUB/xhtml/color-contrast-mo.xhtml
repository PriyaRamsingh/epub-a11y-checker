Color Contrast for Media Overlays

Bad examples of contrast

This page contains bad examples of contrast for both the actively-playing text as well as for the shaded-out inactive items.

As the Media Overlays playback continues, you can see that the highlighted text stands out, but does not have enough contrast between its own foreground and background colors.

The text that's not currently being played also changes style when you press play, but its contrast is too low to pass WCAG level AA.
